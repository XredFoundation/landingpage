Bounty program rules

The amount of distributing tokens for the "bounty" program

In our White paper we have declared that 2% of the total amount of issued tokens will be distributed by a "bounty" program. We have an Escrow agreement, and as stated in our documents, all funds will be returned, if during pre-ICO and ICO we do not reach our 1.5M$ target. Our total cap will be 65M tokens. There are several conditions, we will assume the volume for the bounty program the sum of 2% of the total cap. The sum for the bounty program equals 1 300 000 tokens. All bounty tokens will be distributed during the 2 weeks after a successful ICO.

Social media and referral program (35%)

455 000 tokens on this part. To participate in this opprotunities, need share referral link by social networks, add in bitcointalk signature, etc. When 3 new member will be atrractd by your repsonal link - you will get 3 token. If somebody from attracted participators will purchased tokens, you will get more 5% of this purchased amount.

XRED Foundation support in online discussions (10%)

130 000 tokens on this part. To participate in this opportunity, need create media post on forums and social networks with description about ICO, then send the link on disscusion into one of our channels and we will evaluate how much tokens you are awared. Our dicision will be based on reached views, count of comments and others metriks.

Translations to other languages on Bitcointalk (15%)

195 000 on this part. To participte in this opportunies, need translate our documents and landing page on one of our official languages. (German, Spanish, Franche, Japanese, Korean). If you are native speaker in other langauge, contact with us, we will decide translate or not.

For special support (40%)

520 000 tokens for this part. To participate in thi opportunities, you can offer us your service in audit open source code, writing media post for mass-media. If you have a platform with big traffic, just place article about our ICO and we get our tokens.

About undistributed tokens

If by some reasons, some part of "bounty" program will have undistributed tokens. This part will divide on proportions between other parts. It will mean, for example, that from special cases we will add yet 300 000 undistributed tokens for referral program for example. Then each participator will get no 3 tokens, but 5-7 tokens for 3 new members, for example.
